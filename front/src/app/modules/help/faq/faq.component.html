CTNAS Frequently Asked Questions :

Where can I see the tenants topologies?

Head to the tenants view, and in the displayed list of tenants, you will notice that there is a topology icon "device_hub" clicking on that icon will show the list of topologies of that tenant and you can view them in the editor by clicking on the link "view in editor".

Where can I see my device interfaces and protocols?

There is multiple ways, you can check the device details under the Device Configurations section you will notice two sections Device Interfaces and Device Protocols, another way is to access the interfaces or protocols page seperatly by clicking the menu buttons on the left menu bar.

How to connect my devices?

You must first add a device, make sure to enter a valid IP address otherwise you won't be able to connect to that device neighbors, and assign it to your topology, after that you can head to the devices details view by clicking on the details icon "assignment" or by right clicking on the device in the topology editor and clicking the "view in details" button, upon clicking on that button a popup will show up containing the brief description for that device click on "view in more details" link and you will be redirected to the details of that device page, you can from there click on the "initiate discovery button" after few seconds, you will get a list of all of that device neighbors and you can connect to all or some of them by checking the box next to the device name, then clicking connect; the connect button will add all of these devices to your topology and create the corresponding links in the topology editor however the save button will only save them to your inventory giving you the flexibility to connect to them or not as you wish later on.

How to add the ip addresses of discovered devices?

In the devices view you can click on the edit icon "" of the chosed device, and from there you can update it's ip address and save, you can also go on from there and see it neighbors and connect to them by accessing the concerned device details view.

How to show the startup configuration of my device?

You need to access the device details view, from there under the "Device Configuration" section you can click on the "Startup Configuration" button.

How to show the running configuration of my device?

You need to access the device details view, from there under the "Device Configuration" section you can click on the "Running Configuration" button.

How to see the differences between the startup and running configurations?

You need to access the device details view, from there under the "Device Configuration" you need to make sure that you have the two configurations ready so you can highlight the differences between them if not an alert will lunch saying you can't display configuration out of nothing, if the two configurations are displayed, you can go ahead and check the "highlight differences" checkbox and it will highlight all the differences between the two configurations.

How to update my profile?

Click on the profile link on the bottom left of the side menu bar from there you can update your image, name, email and password, however, note that if the password or email are updated CTNAS will disconnect you and you will need to login with your new credentails.
